七つの大罪

yang diterbitkan di Indonesia dengan judul Seven Deadly Sins, adalah sebuah seri manga shōnen Jepang bergenre fantasi yang ditulis dan diilustrasikan oleh Nakaba Suzuki.

Nanatsu no taizai synopsis

The seven Deadly Sins were an active group of knights in the Britannia region (ブ リ タ ニ ア Buritania), which were disbanded after they were accused of overthrowing the Kingdom of Liones (リ オ ネ ス ス Rionesu Ōkoku). They were said to be defeated at the hands of the Holy Knights, but rumors continued to say that they were still alive. Ten years later, the Holy Knights plot a coup and capture the king, and become the tyrannical rulers of the kingdom. The third princess, Elizabeth, then sets out on a journey to find the Seven Deadly Sins and begs them for help in reclaiming the kingdom from the Holy Knights.
