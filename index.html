フェアリーテイル

Fairy Tail is a Japanese shōnen manga series written and illustrated by Hiro Mashima. The manga was serialized in Weekly Shōnen Magazine from August 2006 to July 2017, and has been published in 63 tankōbon volumes by Kodansha. The story tells of Natsu Dragneel, a member of the popular wizard guild Fairy Tail, who travels to Earth-land in search of a dragon named Igneel.

ブラッククローバー

Black Clover is a Japanese fantasy shōnen manga series written and illustrated by Yūki Tabata. The story tells of a boy named Asta who was born without magic power, an abnormal phenomenon in the world he lives in. Together with his friends from the Black Bulls, he dreams of becoming the Wizard King. The manga has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since February 2015, and has been bundled into twenty-five tankōbon volumes as of July 2020.
