a Japanese shōnen manga series written and illustrated by Tite Kubo.

Bleach synopsis

The story follows the adventures of a stubborn teenager named Ichigo Kurosaki who inherits the destiny of his parents, after he gets the power of a Shinigami (死神 Shinigami, trans. "God of Death") a death personification similar to the Angel of Death of another Shinigami, Rukia Kuchiki. This new power forces him to undertake the task of protecting humans from evil spirits and leading the souls of the dead to the afterlife, and leads him to adventures in the various spiritual realms that exist.
