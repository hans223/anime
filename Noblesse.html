노블레스

an animation by a webtoon written by Jeho Son and drawn by Kwangsu Lee. Starting on December 30, 2007, this webtoon / manhwa was released by Naver.

Noblesse synopsis

tells of a noble clan (nobility / nobility) who is very strong Cadis Etrama Di Raizel (commonly called Rai) - who slept for 820 years without knowing the development of mankind. At the start of the webtoon, Rai awakens in an abandoned building in South Korea, and tries to adapt to the modern world. He went to school, where he met his loyal servant Frankenstein. With Frankenstein's help, Rai enters Ye Ran High School and accidentally becomes friends with Shinwoo, Ikhan, Yuna, and the others. Noblesse focuses on adventurers Rai, Frankenstein, and the others in fighting a secret organization, "Unions".
