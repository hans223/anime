ワンピース

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. The manga has been published in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since July 22, 1997, and has been bundled into 91 tankōbon volumes.

Onepiece synopsis

The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy who has the ability to have an elastic body like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit by accident. With his pirate crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy scoured the Grand Line in search of the world's greatest treasure trove known as "One Piece" in order to become the next Pirate King.
